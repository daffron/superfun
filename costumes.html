More on Facepainting

I love face painting! I've created a very unique point of difference with my face painting which are character designs that go on the cheeks/forehead. Kids can choose exactly what they want from the huge collection of themed designs such as Star Wars, princesses, animals, minions, Mickey Mouse, superheroes, Pokemon... the list goes on!
The other advantage is speed. I'm able to get lots of kids face painting done while maintaining a high level of quality which is really important when having limited time at a party and also doing pure face paint events with a long queue (I don't want kids waiting in line too long!)

More on Superheroes

I love superheroes, always have. I grew up dreaming of being Spiderman and Batman and now I get to live that dream!
Superhero parties are a personal favourite of mine. I am extremely passionate about creating an incredible and "real" superhero experience for kids. A huge part of that is the costume quality.
I am a real perfectionist and I won't settle for less than the best so it's taken me over a year to complete my custom created, sized and pieced together Spiderman and Batman costumes!
I'm always looking to create new themed things to provide for superhero parties and I've already got a great selection such as superhero face painting, balloon sword fighting, hero glitter tattoos, tug of war, acrobatic stunts and music, and my custom created "superhero power detector" which scans kids for superpowers... very exciting and empowering for them!
I also make a themed birthday card to give to the birthday child and give out special superhero "tokens" as prizes.
One of my big projects for a long time has been to do real superhero rope swinging, like Batman and Spiderman do in the movies! I've got years of rock climbing experience and rope swinging is a hobby of mine so I've combined all my skills together to be able to do it for real!
My idea is to be able to make special videos for the birthday child (to be sent to the parent before the party) of Batman/Spiderman swinging from a rope and then letting the birthday child know Batman/Spiderman is looking forward to coming to their upcoming birthday party!
I'm also working on having premium quality costumes for all popular heroes such as Iron Man, Darth Vader, Captain America, Superman etc.

More on Balloon Twisting

Always a hit at parties, balloon animals are a guaranteed way to break the ice and get kids involved!
I've created custom made balloon boxes that are portable and allow quick and effective balloon work!
Ive also created special "carry me" costumes that go perfectly with purely balloon twisting parties as it gives
kids a lot of entertainment while waiting in line for balloons! (Parents are also thoroughly entertained by the costumes!)
