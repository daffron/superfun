Party Services

Each party is customised for your preferences and I always bring a great selection of equipment to keep the party going non stop!
I have lots of options for costumes and themes for kids parties such as pirate, Peter Pan, fireman etc but it's also very easy to buy new costumes if you have a theme that I don't have a costume for!
My games and activities are always evolving and I can bring a large range of equipment such as giant bubbles, indoor giant bubbles where I create a big bubble around each child, tug of war, balloon battles, stencil face painting, races, bullrush style games, spinning plates, juggling etc.
Another really fun range of costumes that I have are "inflatable" costumes. I've currently got a clown, Bumblebee, cow, captain America, shark eating a diver and a T-Rex!
I do regular toddler events where I do activities from music and dancing, bubbles, story time, jingle bells and musical shakers, balloon games, giant parachute etc.
I also do regular holiday programs where I do a large mixture of activities such as bubble art, bookmark making, slime making, cupcake decorating, sprout gardens, pipe cleaner creations and many more to come!
